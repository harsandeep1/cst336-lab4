Welcome to Venus.


This is the second planet in the Solar System and closest to the Earth. Venus was a Roman goddess of love and beauty, and this planet is named after her. Venus is also called the sister planet to Earth because of a few similarities, such as size and mass. It is a terrestrial planet, and its atmosphere is made of 96% Carbon dioxide, which makes it harder for any life-form to exists on this planet. It orbits the Sun in approximately 224 earth days.
